FAQ

Good to know before you visit

Is Relax Beauty Zone only for ladies?+
Yes! We are an exclusively ladies beauty parlour, so you can relax in a completely private and comfortable space.
What are your timings & days?+
We're open 10:30 AM to 9:30 PM, and we're closed on Mondays. Walk-ins are welcome, but booking ahead on WhatsApp avoids waiting.
Do I need an appointment?+
Not mandatory, but recommended — especially for facials, bridal and party makeup. Just message us on WhatsApp with your preferred date & time.
Do you do bridal makeup & mehndi?+
Yes — light, party and HD bridal makeup, hair do, saree draping and bridal/party mehndi. Ask us about our Bridal Glam package.
Where are you located?+
Shop No.6, Vastu Vihar CHS, Desale Pada, Manpada Road, Dombivli East, Thane — 421201. See the map in the footer for directions.
How do I see prices?+
Pricing depends on the service, length/design and products chosen. Message us on WhatsApp and we'll happily share current rates and offers.
